# Contoso — People & Benefits Guide (Plan Year 2026)

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees accrue **20 days** of PTO per year (1.67 days/month).
- Up to **5 unused days** carry over into the next plan year; the rest are forfeited on Dec 31.
- PTO requests are submitted in Workday and require manager approval at least 3 business days in advance for blocks longer than 2 days.
- Sick leave is separate: 10 days/year, no carryover, no manager pre-approval needed.

## Parental Leave
- Birthing parents: 16 weeks fully paid.
- Non-birthing / adoptive parents: 12 weeks fully paid.
- Leave must begin within 12 months of the birth or placement.
- Coordinate with HR (people@contoso.com) and your manager at least 30 days before the planned start.

## Health Insurance
- Three medical plans: HSA-Eligible PPO, Standard PPO, and HMO.
- Open enrollment runs Nov 1–15 each year; changes outside that window require a qualifying life event.
- Contoso contributes $1,500/year to the HSA for employees on the HSA-Eligible PPO.

## 401(k) Retirement
- Contoso matches 100% of the first 4% and 50% of the next 2% of eligible pay.
- Match is immediately vested.
- Enroll or change contributions any time via Fidelity NetBenefits.

## Learning & Development
- Each employee has a **$2,000/year** professional development budget.
- Covers courses, certifications (incl. Microsoft Certifications), books, and conferences.
- Submit reimbursement in Workday > Expenses with the receipt and a one-line business justification.
- Microsoft Learn and LinkedIn Learning are free to all staff and do not draw down the budget.

## Expense Reimbursement
- Submit within **60 days** of the expense date.
- Receipts required for anything over $25.
- Travel must follow the T&E policy; book through Concur to qualify for the negotiated rates.
